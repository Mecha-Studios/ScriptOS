About ScriptOS for Personal Use

This version of ScriptOS is made for developers, home computers, and your average user. The Personal Use version offers top of the line customer support. This version allows for redistribution under terms outlined in the copyright license

Advantages of using ScriptOS

Speed

ScriptOS 5 has shown to be faster than other web operating systems such as Chrome OS.

Power Usage

ScriptOS 5 uses far less power than ChromeOS and is light weight as it is built entirely on JavaScript.
